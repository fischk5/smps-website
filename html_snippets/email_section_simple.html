EXCELLENT CANDIDATES ONLY

The 2024 SMPS Pinnacle Awards ceremony takes place at the Heartland Regional Conference on April 24-26 in Indianapolis. The Pinnacle Award recognizes excellence in marketing communications produced by professional services firms in the design and building industry.

Ready to shine? If your company is involved in the built environment, you have until February 16 to submit your best work (circa 2022) across:

  • Special Events
  • Holiday Pieces
  • Corporate Identity
  • Promotional Campaigns
  • Recruitment & Retention
  • Internal Communications

Don't let imposter syndrome stop you. The Pinnacle Awards are a regional competition which means your odds of winning skyrocket. Start your application here.

Members of SMPS Twin Cities are eligible to apply for the Pinnacle Awards.
