"Don't be afraid to fall forward"

Featured Member: Kevin Fischer

What was your first job growing up?
Stacking the Sunday paper at the Star Tribune. Made one nickel per paper stacked!

What's the most unusual item on your desk or in your office?
I have an age progressed picture of myself on my desk to remind me who I'm ultimately working for while I'm alive.

What's something you're proud of?
Passing the PE exam after leaving the industry. Was a capstone of my first career.

In-person meetings or virtual calls?
In person as much as possible! I have to know what your face looks like when I drop puns or sarcastic remarks.

What professional advice would you give yourself two years ago?
Talk about your work more.

What was the last movie you watched? How was it?
Jurassic Park 3 - Sam Neill is a legend.

What's the most unusual request you've ever gotten from a client?
I'm not making this up. Once got this RFP question: "If your company was a celebrity, who would you be and why?"

What's the best piece of professional advice you've ever received?
Hard work beats talent when talent doesn't work hard.

What's your favorite marketing tool?
Tetheros - no better tool for organizing work across every corner of my professional life.

Kevin is the founder of Tetheros and has been a member of SMPS for 1.5 years.
